Spider-Man Across The Spider-Verse

Following his reunion with Gwen Stacy, Brooklyn's amiable, full-time neighborhood Spider-Man finds himself propelled across the Multiverse and confronted by a group of Spider-People tasked with safeguarding its very survival. However, Miles finds himself up against the other Spiders as the heroes disagree over how to deal with a fresh menace. In order to save the ones he loves the most, he will soon need to redefine what it means to be a hero.
